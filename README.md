# Vehicle Market Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes vehicle registration data to identify:

- Market share performance
- Segment growth trends
- Regional opportunities
- Brand competitiveness
- Customer demographics

## Business Problem

Management required a clear understanding of:

- Which vehicle segments are growing fastest
- Regional sales opportunities
- Market penetration rates
- Competitor positioning

## Solution

Developed a Power BI dashboard that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor market share
- Track registration growth
- Compare brand performance
- Identify untapped market opportunities

## Key Insights

- Bus segment experienced the highest growth rate.
- Commercial vehicles represented the largest market volume.
- Several counties showed strong growth but low company penetration.
- Market share varied significantly across vehicle categories.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Excel
- SQL

## Dashboard Features

- Executive KPI Cards
- Market Share Analysis
- Segment Growth Trends
- Regional Analysis
- Brand Comparison
- Opportunity Mapping

## Business Impact

The dashboard enabled management to identify high-growth opportunities and prioritize sales efforts effectively.
